FAQs

Hull Drainage Questions

Do you help with blocked toilets in Hull?

Yes, this page is for urgent blocked toilets, blocked drains, slow-draining sinks, outside drains and sewage smells across Hull and nearby areas.

Is a blocked outside drain an emergency?

It can be, especially if water or waste is backing up, overflowing, or causing smells near the property.

What should I do while waiting?

Avoid repeatedly flushing, stop running water into the blocked drain if possible, keep children and pets away from wastewater, and take photos if safe.

Do you cover my area?
